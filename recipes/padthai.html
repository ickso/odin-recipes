Pad Thai

Description

Created in the 1930s by thai nationalists, there is nothing traditional or authentic about it. Exists because rice shortage made the gov force the noodle meme. You're gonna love it, fatty.

Ingredients

Main

  • rice noodles
  • eggs
  • protein of choice
    • chicken
    • shrimp
    • tofu
    • whatever
  • garlic
  • onion
  • peanuts
  • bean sprouts
  • oil

Sauce

  • tamarind paste 30g
  • soy sauce 30g
  • fish sauce 20g
  • brown sugar 40g
  • half a lime

Steps

Pre

  1. Marinate protein in soy sauce for however long you wish
  2. Soak rice noodles in hot (but not boiling) water for ~15min
  3. Whisk the sauce together - add tamarind paste 30g, soy sauce 30g. fish sauce 20g, brown sugar 40g and juice from half a lime and a tablespoon of water if too thick

Main

  1. Fry onion until soft, add garlic after a minute or two
  2. Add the protein, time and temp depends on type
  3. Add egg, scramble
  4. Add bean sprouts, whatever vegetables you decided on and peanuts
  5. Add drained noodles
  6. Dump sauce in and mix well
  7. If too thick add some noodle soak water
  8. Leave to simmer for a couple minutes, until noodles are of desired softness
  9. Sprinkle some peanuts, chives and uncooked bean sprouts for some crunch
